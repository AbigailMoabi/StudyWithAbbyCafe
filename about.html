Who We Are

My name is Abigail Moabi, founder and lead developer of Study With Abby Café.

A library is a great place to study but has some strict rules. For instance, you are not allowed to eat nor discuss within the premises. Sometimes that makes it hard for one to focus for long hours.

The café was born from the idea to make studying long lasting and enjoyable. The café provides comfortable space for studying, allows for discussions and one can order what to eat to keep the body fueled up and the mind active.

At Study With Abby Café we value good conduct to ensure customer satisfaction and are committed to offer the best services throughout.
